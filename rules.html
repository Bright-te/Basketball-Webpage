Basketball Rules

Objective of the Game

Score more than the other team by shooting the ball through the opponent's basket

Players

Each team has 5 players on the court at a time

Scoring

Free throw gives the player a point, a throw inside the three-point line is two points and outside the line gives you three-points.

Dribbling

A player must dribble(bounce) the ball while moving

Double Dribble

One cannot stop dribbling and then start dribbling again

Out of bounds

The ball is out of bounds if it touches the boundary line, and possession goes to the team

Fouls

No pushing, holding, hitting, tripping, or illegal contact with an opponent
